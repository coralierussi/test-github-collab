What's in store for you during this challenge ?

An experience based on fun, learning and practice

We hope that your week will be filled with learning and hands-on experience, but most of all that you will have lots of fun and enjoyment!

The basics of serious games through videos and quizzes

A team of serious game experts will join you in the Eneglish Game to share their experience and teach you the fundamentals of game mechanics.

Tools to design a project from A to Z

Each day will be based on a design process. From Monday to Friday, you will be supported as you design a project: define the concept, the level design, and the prototype, right up to producing th efinal game to present for the national competition.
